Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025.

Your privacy matters. Below we explain what data we may collect when you use our free, play-for-fun games and how we use it. The site does not involve real money or prizes of cash value.

Data we may collect

When you browse the site, we may receive technical data your browser sends by default (e.g. IP address, browser and device type, pages visited). You can play without creating an account. If you email us or use the contact form, we receive whatever you submit (name, email, message) so we can reply.

Cookies and local storage

We may use cookies or local storage to remember settings, save in-game progress (e.g. chip balance), or improve your experience. This data stays on your device. You can clear or block cookies in your browser; doing so may affect how games or the site work and may reset in-game data.

Analytics

We may use analytics to see how the site is used (e.g. which pages get traffic, session length). That helps us fix problems and improve the experience. Data is usually aggregated and not tied to you personally unless you have given us identifying information (e.g. by contacting us).

Third parties

We may use services (e.g. analytics) that collect technical or usage data under their own policies. We do not sell or rent your personal information.

How we use your messages

Contact form and email submissions are used only to answer you. We do not use your details for marketing unless you opt in. We do not sell your data.

How long we keep data

We retain data only as long as necessary (e.g. to reply to you or run the site). Data in your browser (e.g. game state) stays on your device until you clear it or the app overwrites it.

Questions or requests

For privacy questions or to ask what data we hold about you, use the Contact page or email contact@spinwaves.site. We will respond as required by law.
