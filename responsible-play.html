Responsible Play

Our games are for casual, low-stakes fun. They’re simulated, run in the browser, and use play money only—no real cash, no prizes of value.

Below are simple ways to keep play enjoyable and balanced.

Play for fun

Treat the games as light entertainment. If you find yourself playing for reasons other than enjoyment, take a step back.

Take breaks

Step away from the screen from time to time. Long sessions when you’re tired or distracted are rarely as much fun.

Set a time limit

Decide in advance how long you’ll play and try to stick to it. Mixing game time with other activities helps keep things in balance.

Play money only

All chips and credits here are fictional. They can’t be exchanged for cash or anything else. You can’t win anything of real value.

Simulated, not real

Cards, reels, dice, and wheels are simulated for amusement. They are not real and don’t offer real-money outcomes.

Adults only (18+)

This site is for people 18 and over. We don’t offer real-money and don’t aim our services at anyone younger. If you’re under 18, please don’t use the site.

If you have questions or want to talk about how you’re playing, get in touch. We’re happy to help.
